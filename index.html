My Projects

Carbon Footprint Tracker

The Carbon Footprint Tracker is a tool designed to help individuals and organizations monitor and reduce their environmental impact. By tracking daily activities such as energy consumption, transportation, and waste, the project calculates the amount of carbon dioxide emissions produced. Users receive personalized feedback and suggestions to lower their carbon footprint, encouraging sustainable habits. This project combines data analysis with user-friendly design to promote environmental awareness and support the global effort against climate change.

SCM

SCM is a secure text messaging website designed to provide users with private and encrypted communication. With end-to-end encryption, SCM ensures that messages are only readable by the sender and the intended recipient, protecting conversations from eavesdropping or unauthorized access. The platform features a simple and intuitive interface, supporting real-time messaging, group chats, and multimedia sharing while prioritizing user privacy and data security. Whether for personal or professional use, SCM offers a trustworthy solution for confidential conversations in today’s digital world.
